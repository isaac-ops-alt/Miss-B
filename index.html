Fufu and Eru is a iconic, nutritious Cameroonian dish consisting of nutrient-dense, finely shredded leafy green soup (Eru) paired with a soft, fermented cassava dough (Water Fufu). Pricing Transparency

What Shapes Your Quote?

Every event is different, so every quote is personalised. Here's what we consider when building your proposal:

Guest Count

The number of guests is the primary factor. More guests means more ingredients, more staff, and longer service time.

Cuisine & Menu

The number of dishes, cuisine types, and premium ingredients chosen all influence the final cost.

Service Hours

Extended staffing, multi-day events, or late-night service are priced to reflect the additional time and team.

Travel & Location

We serve all of England. Events outside Leicester may incur a travel fee, always disclosed upfront in your quote.

Décor Bundle

Adding event décor and styling to your catering package is available at a combined rate, often offering better overall value.

Tableware & Equipment

Premium china, glassware hire, and specialist equipment are optional add-ons. Disposables are included in all base packages.

Booking Policy

How We Handle Your Booking

Deposit
A deposit is required to secure your booking date. The exact amount is stated clearly in your personalised quote. Your date is held exclusively for you once the deposit is received and confirmed.

Minor changes to guest numbers, menu, or décor preferences can usually be accommodated up to 14 days before the event, subject to availability. Please notify us as early as possible.
Cancellations
Cancellation terms are outlined in full in your booking agreement. Cancellations well in advance may be eligible for a partial deposit refund, minus costs already incurred. Event insurance is strongly recommended.

About Miss B

Passion, Flavour &
Proud Heritage

Miss B Catering Services was founded in Leicester with a bold and simple vision: to bring the very best of African, Caribbean, and English cuisine to celebrations and events across England — and to do it beautifully.

What began as a passion for cooking for family and friends grew into a trusted catering and events business, serving families, communities, and organisations from Leicester to London and everywhere in between.

We believe food is more than sustenance — it's how cultures celebrate, connect, and create lasting memories. Three food traditions, one team, one standard of excellence.
